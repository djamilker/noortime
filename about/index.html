📖 About NoorTime

NoorTime is a simple project with a powerful goal: to help Muslims around the world stay spiritually connected through tools that are beautiful, accurate, and accessible. We believe that simplicity can be powerful — and faith can be supported by modern design.

🧰 What We Offer

  • 📿 Location-based Prayer Times (AlAdhan API powered)
  • 📆 Accurate Hijri–Gregorian Converter using Umm al-Qura
  • 🗓 Full Islamic Calendar for 1446 AH with major dates
  • 🌙 Real-time Ramadan Countdown and Fasting Times
  • 🧠 All pages designed to be fast, clean, and mobile-friendly

💡 Why We Built NoorTime

As Muslims, we often need quick access to basic tools like prayer times or Hijri dates — but most websites are either cluttered or full of ads. NoorTime was made to fix that: smooth UI, focused tools, and no distractions.

🌍 For the Ummah, By the Ummah

NoorTime is 100% free and always will be. This project was built as a sadaqah jariyah — a continuous charity. You can support us by sharing the site with others, and making du‘a for its success, insha’Allah.
