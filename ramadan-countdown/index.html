What is Ramadan?

Ramadan is the ninth month of the Islamic calendar, a time of fasting, reflection, and community. Muslims around the world abstain from food, drink, and other physical needs from dawn until sunset to grow spiritually and strengthen their connection with Allah.

  • 🕋 Fasting from Suhoor to Iftar
  • 📖 Increased recitation of Qur'an
  • 💫 Seeking Laylat al-Qadr (Night of Power)
  • 🎉 Culminates in Eid al-Fitr celebration
